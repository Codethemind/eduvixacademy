❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Browse through our most commonly asked questions.

What is NIOS and how can Eduvix Academy help me?+
NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling) is India's largest open school that allows students to complete their 10th or 12th education flexibly. Eduvix Academy provides complete registration support, study materials, recorded classes, and exam guidance for NIOS programs.
Can I enroll if I have failed my 10th or 12th exams?+
Absolutely! Eduvix Academy has specialized programs for students who have failed their board exams. Through NIOS, you can give exams for only the subjects you failed (Transfer of Credit / TOC) without repeating the entire year.
Are the Degree programs valid and recognized?+
Yes, all Degree, PG, and Diploma programs offered through Eduvix Academy are UGC-recognized and from accredited universities. They are equivalent to regular degrees and accepted for government and private jobs.
How do I attend classes? Are they online?+
We offer a hybrid learning model — recorded video lectures you can watch anytime, live doubt-clearing sessions, digital study materials, and WhatsApp-based personal support. You can study from anywhere in Kerala or India.
What are the fees and are there any scholarships?+
Fees vary by program and are very affordable. We also offer flexible installment payment options. Contact our counselors for detailed fee information and to know about any applicable scholarship schemes.
How quickly can I get admission?+
Admission is quick and simple! Contact us via WhatsApp or fill the inquiry form, and our counselors will guide you through the entire process. Admission for NIOS is open throughout the year with multiple admission cycles.
