📋 Detailed Steps

Your Admission Roadmap

📞
Step 1

Contact Us

Reach out to us via WhatsApp, phone call, email, or by filling our online inquiry form. Our team responds within 2 hours during working hours.

🗣️
Step 2

Free Counseling Session

Our expert counselors will understand your academic background, goals, and recommend the best program. This session is completely free with no obligation.

📝
Step 3

Registration & Documents

Submit required documents and complete the online registration process. Our team assists you with every form and documentation requirement.

✅
Step 4

Admission Confirmation

Receive your official admission confirmation, enrollment number, and student ID. You're now officially a part of the Eduvix family!

📚
Step 5

Begin Learning with Full Support

Access your study materials, recorded classes, assignment support, and WhatsApp helpline. Your academic journey has officially begun!

📄 Documents Required

Keep these documents ready to make your admission process smooth and quick.

Aadhaar Card
Previous Marksheets
Passport Size Photos
Transfer Certificate (TC)
Caste Certificate (if applicable)
Conduct Certificate
ID Proof (PAN / Voter ID)
Address Proof

💡 Note: Don't worry if you're missing some documents. Our counselors will guide you on how to obtain them or if any alternative documents are acceptable for your specific program.
