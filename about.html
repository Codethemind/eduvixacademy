📖 Our Story

The Eduvix Journey

Eduvix Academy was founded with a single, powerful belief — that every student deserves a second chance and a clear path forward. Too many talented young people in Kerala were missing out on education due to rigid systems, failed exams, or lack of guidance.

We started as a small counseling center in Kerala, guiding students through NIOS registration and exam support. As word spread about our results and our approach, we grew — adding degree programs, career courses, digital resources, and a team of dedicated mentors.

Today, Eduvix Academy proudly serves over 5,000 students across Kerala with programs ranging from NIOS 10th & 12th to full Distance Degree and professional career certifications.
